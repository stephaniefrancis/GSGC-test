Weekly Gospel Choir Mass

Sunday, 7.30pm at St. Francis Xavier Church, Gardiner Street Upper, Dublin 1

Each Sunday evening from late September to early June (excluding bank holiday weekends), you will catch us singing at the 7.30pm mass, where we love to get the congregation singing and dancing along with us. You are sure to leave the church revived and ready for the week ahead!

Everyone is welcome to our weekly celebration and don't forget to hang around for the cuppa & chat in the hall afterwards.

It's only a 15 min walk from O'Connell Street or you can get to us using the following Dublin Bus routes: 1, 11, 13, 16, 33, 40B/D, 41, 122.
