Joining Gardiner Street Gospel Choir

We have just finished auditions for the current 2013/2014 choir year. There is a waiting list for the next round of auditions, please feel free to add your name via email if you think you are interested in joining in August/September of 2014.

If you're interested in joining, please also read about what's involved in being a member of the choir.

If you're happy with all of this and feel you can give this level of commitment, then please drop us an email to Join@GardinerStreetGospelChoir.com with your contact details and any other helpful information about you.

We will reply to your email as soon as possible to let you know that you have been added to the membership waiting list.

Thanks for your interest!

The GSGC Team.

What's involved

  • The choir year runs from late August to late June.
  • Weekly practice every Wednesday 7.30-9.30pm, changing to 8.00-9.30pm in the second half of your first year.
  • Weekly evening Mass every Sunday with a 1 hour rehearsal beforehand (6.30-8.30pm).
  • Extra gigs/performances throughout the year, potentially with extra practices in the lead up to these events.
  • There is no gospel mass on bank holiday weekends with the exception of Easter Sunday.
