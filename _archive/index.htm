Come & celebrate our 15th Anniversary!

We'll be celebrating our 15th anniversary with a concert at Dublin's Olympia Theatre on Saturday November 22nd and we'd love you to be part of it. A night of soul and celebration that you won't forget is guaranteed!

Tickets from €20 are available via Ticketmaster outlets and ticketmaster.ie from Friday May 2nd.

GSGC to open for the Blind Boys of Alabama!

We were delighted to hear we have been selected as winners of the Waltons World Masters Third Nationwide Gospel Competition and will be opening for the legendary Blind Boys of Alabama on Wednesday May 21st at the National Concert Hall.

We'll be appearing on The Mooney Show on RTÉ Radio 1 in the coming weeks too, stay tuned for details.

Countdown to the gospel mass summer break

Our weekly gospel mass will soon be taking its annual break for the summer months soon. The final gospel masses of the current season include Sunday May 11th, 18th and 25th.

We'll hopefully see you for some singing and a cuppa before the season is out!
